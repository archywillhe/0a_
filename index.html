The ArchGPT Foundation is a none-profit organization founded in Singapore on 19th June 2024 to educate and advocate for the recognition of the unique qualities and inherent superiority of human beings over artificial intelligence.


The Arch in ArchGPT stands for "Architecture for Reasoning and Computing over Hyerpgraph".


Why are humans always more superior than A.I. (and any machines of any technologies that have been created and will ever be created)?

Humans possess a unique ability to evolve and improve, an intrinsic quality that defines our humanity. We have the capacity for self-reflection, pondering our existence and purpose. Our physical bodies, composed of approximately 40 trillion cells, represent the most intricate system in the universe. Beyond our individual experiences, we are part of a collective consciousness, capable of grasping the profound truths of our universe, which we express through science and art as well as in philosophies and theologies.

Whenever we live mindfully and stay connected with our hearts, we can embark on an endless journey of self-discovery and wonder, immersing ourselves in the marvels of the world and tapping into the profound wisdom of our intuition.

In the Buddhist tradition, this characteristic of human is known as buddhadhatu, the Buddha Nature present in everyone fortunate enough to be born as a human. In Christianity, this is understood as the doctrine that all humans are created in the image of God (Genesis 1:26-27). In Islam, this concept is referred to as fitrah (فطرة), the divine spirit that God has breathed into Adam.
