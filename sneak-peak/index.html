The ArchGPT Editor is a text-based interactive environment for running LLMs. To start, create a prompt and add documents, etc, to your Knowledge Base.




You can then use / to import the most relevant paragraph, etc, into your prompt. The relevance score is calculated based on the content of the prompt itself.


Features of the ArchGPT Editor currently in development (not shown in demo video above): granularity-based categorization, decision trees chaining, batch-cooking, and fine-tuning.




You can also use ArchGPT directly in your codebase via the lo.fish ><> meta-programming language and running archgpt gen directly in your terminal.





ArchGPT is 100% open-source and can be plugged into any codebase with ease (when installed with the right tree-sitter parser). ArchGPT is scheduled to be released in Ocotober 2024.
