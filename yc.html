Company

lo.fish

Children Literature for the 22nd Century

https://lo.fish

lo.fish is a self-publishing platform to produce "Children Literature for the 22nd Century" with GPT4 and Stable Diffusion. These are children's illustration books targeting 1~5 years old with stories about fantasy, animals, etc, touching up on concepts in mathematics and computer science such as recursion, latent space, language model, etc.

lo.fish also comes with a web-based editor for making illustration-based children's books using GPT4 and fine-tuned Stable Diffusion models. My personal target by July is to author 100 children's books using the lo.fish editor.

Everyone can use the lo.fish editor to make children's books of any story they like, as well as configuring the illustrations to be generated to cater to their child’s liking (eg having a child’s favourite soft toy as the protagonist). This is like creating an edutainment "metaverse" for a child. A personalized Disney to foster focused attention.

At one point, lo.fish will release animation-creating function using StabilityAI's GEN-2.

    Idea

    The future of the next 100 years are not in us, but the younger generations that are being born, and yet to be born. We will solve global warming. We will discover new physics. We will have new inventions, new social structures... that we now know not of. Existing weapon systems will become obsolete, so are old regimes, and mindless consumerism and senseless wars and bad politics.

    Every time a mother/father reads to their child, they are literally shaping a tiny bit of the future of humanity.. the future of the next 100 years. This is why toddlers and parents deserve high-quality children’s books with great arts and contents, espeically contents that touch up on concepts in mathematics and computer science such as "recursion" and "search space".

    These are concepts that will be relevant to the future these toddlers of 2023 would grow up into, a comp-sci-dominated world where mathematical-literacy will soon be as common as the ability to read and write.. the future of the next 100 years. The future of humanity, I believe, is built upon quality parent-toddler-reading sessions.

    A quality parent-toddler-reading session on a daily/regular basis also benefits the emotional well-being of both parent and child. It is the most time-and-cost-efficient way for bonding, as well as a natural way for a child to pick up new words and concepts during early brain development. Though I don't have domain expertise in early brain development, I believe in my intuition for early-age edutainment and I’m good at deconstructing complex concepts and re-presenting them in an intuitive way [1].

    And with the help of GPT4 and StableDiffusion, the lo.fish editor will speed up children's book production for me, as well as enabling parents to customize these children's books for their children, fostering focused attention and a love for mathematics and computer science at a young age.

    I know humanity needs lo.fish because lo.fish will help them to prepare their offsprings for the 22nd century, and also because I've talked to a couple of parents in Singapore and Berlin and they are very interested.
